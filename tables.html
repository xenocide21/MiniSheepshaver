Commands
Command Description
Party List
/add <name> Party Play. Adds a player to your party.
/remove <name> Party Play. Removes a player from your party.
/sharing on Party Play. Enables party sharing (quests).
/sharing off Party Play. Disables party sharing.
General
/get Pick up all items that you are standing on.
/insult Generates a random Shakespearean insult.
/sound on Turns sound effects on.
/sound off Turns sound effects off.
/sound toggle Toggles sound effects.
/music on Turns music on.
/music off Turns music off.
/music toggle Toggles music.
Skills
/<skill name> Use the skill that follows the slash (/). Ex. /lumberjacking
/f<1-15> /<skill> Assigns the skill to a function key. Ex. /f2 /hiding
/f<1-15> <phrase> Assigns a spoken word or phrase to a function key. Ex. /f14 Attack
Pet List
/pet<1-5> <name> Names a pet (see Pets window for numbering). Ex. /pet1 Fido
Attack! Pet will attack any creature you target for attack.
Claim <name>! Removes your pet from the stable. You must be at a city/town or guild hall stable.
Release! Pet will disappear and return to the wild.
Guard! Pet will attack any creature it sees attacking you and defend itself against attack.
Heel! Pet will come to you and remain at your side not defend itself against attack.
Move! Pet will move from it's current location.
Speak! Pet will make its characteristic noise.
Stable! Puts your pet in the stable. You must be at a city/town or guild hall stable.
Stay! Pet will remain in place and not defend itself against attack.
